Next step for the Metastatic Prostate Cancer Project: we need some additional information from you
-ddp.salutation-
Thank you for enrolling in the Metastatic Prostate Cancer Project and giving us your consent for research. Your participation will allow us to obtain copies of your medical records, which we will use to verify your eligibility for this study. We will also mail you a kit with instructions on how to provide us a sample of your saliva. After we have reviewed your medical records, we may request a small portion of your stored tumor sample from the hospital where it is kept. After we have analyzed your samples, we will return any unused tumor samples to the pathology department that sent them to us.
Before we can proceed, we will need your mailing address and some information about your physician(s) and hospital(s). Please click on the link below to provide this information:

Contact Info Form

If you have questions at any point and would like to talk to a member of our study staff, please reach out to us at info@mpcproject.org or call 651-293-5029.

Thank you for being part of the Leiomyosarcoma Project. As a part of the project, we are partnering with Invitae in order to share information with participants about their germline DNA (or “normal” DNA).

We are now asking you to opt in if you would like to take part in this additional aspect of the study. Taking part in this is completely optional and will not have any impact on your overall participation in the LMSproject.

The following steps are involved with participation in this additional aspect of the study:

  • Sign an additional consent form
  • Schedule and have a telehealth appointment with a genetic counselor from Invitae or their telehealth partner, Genome Medical
  • Provide an additional saliva sample to Invitae (a kit will be sent to you)
  • Schedule and have a second telehealth appointment with a genetic counselor to discuss the results and any questions you may have.

If you elect to participate, we will share your name, address, and contact information with Invitae to conduct this process. If you choose not to participate in this process, your data will not be shared with Invitae.

If you are interested in participating in this optional aspect of the study, please sign the additional consent form called “Consent Addendum – Learn more about your germline DNA” found in your participant dashboard.
